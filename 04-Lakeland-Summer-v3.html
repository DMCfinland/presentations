Järvisydän Resort

In the heart of Saimaa — Finland's most celebrated lake region — Järvisydän is a full-service resort offering world-class accommodation, an award-winning cave spa, top-10 dining, and a complete range of lakeland activities.

From glass-roofed Scenery Suites to lakeside Kuru Lake Suites and floating houseboats, every room type frames the water and forest. The resort serves as our primary Lakeland hub, with all experiences and excursions departing from one beautiful location.

Järvisydän welcomes groups from intimate couples' retreats to incentive programmes of 200 guests — the same seamless quality at every scale.

Award-Winning
Spa & Wellness

Voted Best Spa in Finland multiple times, Järvisydän's wellness offering is built around the unique combination of underground stone and open lake.

Cave Spa — Finland's first cave spa, carved into natural rock. Turquoise pools, log-and-stone architecture, and an atmosphere unlike any other wellness space in the Nordics.

Lake Spa — Outdoor pools overlooking Saimaa. Traditional smoke sauna, cold plunge pools, and peat treatments using local ingredients. A holistic journey from underground stone to open sky.

Dining

Solitary by Kuru — Top 10 restaurant in Finland. An intimate dining concept where the chef, the ingredients, and the landscape are all within arm's reach. The ultimate Saimaa culinary experience.

Tulimenu — Our signature fire menu experience. Seasonal ingredients cooked over open flame at your table — an interactive dining moment that brings the Finnish campfire tradition indoors.

Kota Restaurant Savonloitsu — The resort's iconic teepee restaurant. Open-flame cooking under a traditional kota roof, smoked meats, and Finnish campfire cuisine.

Dinner Cruise — A floating restaurant on the Saimaa waterway. Multi-course dinner as islands and forests glide past at sunset.

Tahko Resort

Finland's most versatile resort destination, set in Northern Savonia. Tahko delivers year-round adventure — winter skiing and snowsports meet summer golf, cycling, and water activities — all against a backdrop of rolling lakeland hills.

Summer & Autumn: 200 km of cycling and mountain-bike trails, championship golf, lake cruises, hiking, kayaking, and guided nature experiences. The forested hills and open water create a contrast that changes completely with the seasons.

Winter: 23 ski slopes, cross-country tracks, fat biking on frozen lake, and snowshoeing through silent birch forest. Ice fishing on Nilsiänjärvi lake.

Accommodation: Resort hotels, private villas, and lakeside cabins. Group capacity from small incentive teams to large conference programmes.

Sahalahti

A peaceful lakeside retreat in Puumala, on the shores of Lake Saimaa. Sahalahti embodies traditional Finnish countryside at its most authentic — rolling farmland, mirror-calm bays, and a rhythm of life measured by the seasons.

For clients who want the full Finnish countryside experience, Sahalahti offers a genuine alternative to resort-based programmes. The intimacy here is the point — small groups, private villas, and experiences you cannot find on any booking platform.

Activities: Lake swimming from private docks, rowing across morning-mist bays, berry and mushroom picking in season, traditional lakeside sauna rituals, and slow forest walks. Evening bonfire by the water is a standard, not an upsell.

Accommodation: Traditional Finnish villas and lakeside cottages sleeping 8–30 guests. Fully catered or self-catering. Ideal for executive retreats, intimate incentive groups, and private family travel.
