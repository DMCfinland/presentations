FINLAND DMC DOs AND DON'Ts
===========================
(Updated: Week 1 Starter | Rules: 10 | Source: Patrick's knowledge)
(Add new rules from Friday review feedback below the line)

DO:
1. Always mention specific Finnish experiences (midnight sun, sauna, aurora, lakes) relevant to the client's travel season
2. Reference the client's group size, dates, and stated preferences in the first paragraph — show you read their email
3. Suggest 2-3 specific activities with brief, compelling descriptions — make them imagine being there
4. Include clear next steps in every email (what you need from them, what happens next, timeline)
5. Personalize heavily for returning clients — reference past trips, known preferences, previous group details found in M365

DON'T:
6. Never promise specific weather conditions — say "typical" or "usually" and frame weather positively ("even on cloudy nights, the atmosphere is magical")
7. Never quote exact fixed prices in initial inquiry responses — use "from EUR X" or "typically EUR X-Y per person" until a formal proposal
8. Never use generic filler phrases like "Finland has something for everyone" or "we offer a wide range of services" — be specific
9. Never forget transfer and logistics details for remote locations — clients worry about getting there
10. Never send without verifying: did you answer ALL the client's questions? Missing even one creates a follow-up cycle
